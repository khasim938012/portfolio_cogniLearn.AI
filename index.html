The Problem

Fragmented Learning

Students switch between 5+ tabs: ChatGPT, YouTube, Notion, and Compilers. Focus is lost in the chaos.

Language Barriers

High-quality tech education is 90% English. Brilliant regional minds are left behind due to the language gap.

Lack of Mentorship

Video courses are passive. They don't adapt if you miss a day, and they can't conduct a mock interview.

The Solution

Unified Intelligent Dashboard

CogniLearn replaces the chaos with a single, voice-controlled ecosystem. It understands your native language, tracks your progress, and builds your career tools for you.

  • Native Language Support (Hindi, Kannada, Tamil, Telugu)
  • Dynamic Study Plans that adjust to your pace
  • Interactive Resume Builder & Mock Interviews

Everything You Need
To Succeed

CogniLearn isn't just a chatbot. It's a suite of powerful tools designed to take you from learning to earning.

Multilingual Voice
